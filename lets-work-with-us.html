The Adulis Story.

efore the company, before the execution, before the idea, it was two individuals. In 2016, inspired by the lack of transparency in the electronic buyback industry, the abysmal trade-in values of carriers, and the lack of customer convenience, we dug our heads in the groundwork. What we found reaffirmed our belief that customers were being manipulated, and swindled of a great value opportunity. Carriers in almost cartel like fashion set prices for products and plans, lock us in to two year financing agreements to sidestep competition, and undervalue trade-ins as they actualize on the surprising growth of the used smartphone market. The customers needs is left out of the equation, and it’s only getting worse with the consolidation in the industry.
